Description

The Diavola pizza is said to originate from the Campania region of Italy where there are a wide variety of spiced charcuterie.

The name Diavola translates as the devilâ€™s pizza and is one of the most popular pizzas around the world loved for its fiery heat.

Ingredients

  • 150 ml San Marzano Tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon Parmesan
  • 60 g Fior Di Latte Mozzarella
  • 6 Slices of Spicy Salami
  • 1 Meaty Pork Sausage
  • 1 Handful of Kalamata Olives
  • 2 tablespoons Chili Flakes
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Spread the tomatoes on the pizza to form a base
  2. Break the spicy salami into pieces on the pizza.
  3. Remove the pork sausage from the case and crumble the meat over the pizza.
  4. Add chili flakes to taste. (If you prefer less spicy, skip the flakes)
  5. Add the black olives and sprinkle with olive oil
  6. Add the mozzarella and sprinkle with parmesan cheese
  7. Bake to perfection in the Revolve for 70 seconds.

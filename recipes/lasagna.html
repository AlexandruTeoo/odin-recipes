Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's start with the ingredients:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato products
    • 1 can of crushed tomatos
    • 2 cans of tomato sauce
    • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • Spices & seasoning
    • Fresh parsley
    • Dried basil leaves
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricottaa
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers. Then top with the remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
